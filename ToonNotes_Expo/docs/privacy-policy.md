# ToonNotes Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2024

## Overview

ToonNotes is a note-taking app designed for anime and webtoon fans. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally
- **Notes and content**: All your notes are stored locally on your device
- **App settings**: Theme preferences and customization options
- **Designs**: Custom note designs you create

### Data Processed by Third Parties
When you use AI-powered features (optional), the following data may be sent to Google's Gemini AI service:
- Images you upload for design generation
- Text content you choose to analyze for style suggestions

This data is processed in real-time and is not stored by ToonNotes. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for their data handling practices.

## What We Don't Collect
- We do not require account creation
- We do not collect personal information (name, email, location)
- We do not track your usage or behavior
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
- We do not sell any data to third parties

## Data Storage
All note data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not have access to your notes or the ability to recover them if deleted.

## Third-Party Services
- **Google Gemini AI**: Used for optional AI-powered design features
- **Apple App Store**: For app distribution and in-app purchases (if applicable)

## Children's Privacy
ToonNotes does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact Us
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@toonnotes.app

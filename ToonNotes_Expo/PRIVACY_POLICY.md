# ToonNotes Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 1, 2026**

## Overview

ToonNotes is a note-taking app that respects your privacy. Your notes and personal data stay on your device.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored on Your Device Only
- Notes (title, content, labels)
- App preferences (dark mode, default note color)
- In-app purchase records

This data is stored locally using your device's secure storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

### Optional Data You Provide
- **Gemini API Key**: If you choose to use AI-powered design features, you may enter your own Google Gemini API key. This key is stored securely on your device using iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences.

### Data Sent to Third Parties
- **Google Gemini API**: When you use AI design features, images you select are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). No images are stored by ToonNotes.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not have user accounts or cloud sync
- We do not sell any data to third parties

## Photo Library Access
ToonNotes requests access to your photo library solely to let you select images for AI-powered design creation. Selected images are processed by Google's Gemini API and are not stored or transmitted elsewhere.

## Camera Access
ToonNotes may request camera access to capture images for design creation. These images are processed the same way as photo library images.

## In-App Purchases
Purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. ToonNotes does not have access to your payment information.

## Children's Privacy
ToonNotes does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

## Appendix

### A. Retention Calculation Methodology

- **Classic Retention**: Users active on exactly day N
- **Rolling Retention**: Users active on day N or any day after
- **Bounded Retention**: Users active within a window around day N

This report uses **Classic Retention** unless otherwise noted.

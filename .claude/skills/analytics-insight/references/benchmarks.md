## Seasonal Adjustments

### Expected Variations

| Period | Engagement | Retention | Notes |
|--------|------------|-----------|-------|
| New Year (Jan) | +20-30% | +5-10% | Productivity goals |
| Summer (Jun-Aug) | -10-15% | -5% | Vacation season |
| Back to School (Sep) | +15-25% | +5% | Study usage |
| Holidays (Dec) | -15-20% | -5% | Lower activity |
| Weekends | -20-30% | N/A | Normal pattern |

### Adjustment Factors

When comparing metrics:
- **MoM**: Account for month length (28-31 days)
- **YoY**: Account for seasonal patterns
- **Weekly**: Compare same day of week

## Benchmark Interpretation Guide

### When to Be Concerned

| Metric | Red Flag | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| D1 Retention | <20% | Review onboarding |
| D7 Retention | <10% | Review value prop |
| DAU/MAU | <10% | Investigate engagement |
| Conversion | <1% | Review monetization |
| Onboarding | <50% | Simplify flow |

### When to Celebrate

| Metric | Great Performance | Status |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| D1 Retention | >40% | Excellent onboarding |
| D7 Retention | >25% | Strong value prop |
| DAU/MAU | >25% | High engagement |
| Conversion | >5% | Effective monetization |

### Comparison Guidelines

1. **Compare to baseline first** - Before industry benchmarks
2. **Track trends over time** - Direction matters more than absolute
3. **Segment comparisons** - Free vs Pro, iOS vs Android
4. **Cohort comparisons** - Week over week by signup cohort

# ToonNotes v1.0.0 Release Notes

**Release Date:** December 15, 2025
**Type:** Major Release (Initial Launch)
**Platforms:** iOS

---

## Overview

The initial release of ToonNotes - aesthetic note-taking for webtoon and anime fans.

---

## Features

### AI Design Generation
- Upload any image to create a custom note theme
- AI extracts colors, patterns, and mood
- Powered by Google Gemini

### Auto-Styled Notes
- Every note gets a unique aesthetic design
- 20 free auto-styled notes per month
- Colors, borders, and textures automatically applied

### Note Organization
- Create and manage labels
- Filter notes by label
- Archive and restore notes

### Custom Borders
- 6 free border styles
- Apply to any note
- Mix with textures

### Paper Textures
- Multiple background textures
- Paper, comic, artistic styles
- Adjustable opacity

### Note Sharing
- Export notes as images
- ToonNotes watermark on free tier
- Share via web link

---

## Marketing Focus

**Campaign:** Launch Campaign
**Target:** Webtoon and anime fans (16-35)
**Message:** "Notes that match your aesthetic"

### Key Angles
1. First note app designed for fandom aesthetics
2. AI creates themes from your favorite anime/webtoon
3. No design skills needed

---

## Technical Details

- **Framework:** React Native / Expo
- **AI:** Google Gemini for design generation
- **Storage:** Local-first with SwiftData
- **Platforms:** iOS 15.0+

---

## Known Limitations (v1.0.0)

- No cloud sync (local only)
- No authentication
- Watermark on all exports
- 20 note/month limit
- 1 AI design lifetime

v1.1.0 will add Sign in with Apple for cloud-ready architecture.

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Do
- Empathize with the pain ("We all do this...")
- Show transformation, not just features
- Use real scenarios, not abstract benefits
- Be relieved, not preachy
- Keep it conversational

### Don't
- Guilt-trip about productivity
- Over-promise AI capabilities
- Use jargon (no "leverage", "optimize", etc.)
- Compare to competitors directly
- Make it feel like work

### Brand Voice
**Tone:** Warm, understanding, gently encouraging
**Perspective:** "Your notes want to help you. We just help them do it."

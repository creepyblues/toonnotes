## TikTok Content Strategy

TikTok requires a different approach than Instagram/Twitter:
- **Hook in first 1-3 seconds** (or they scroll)
- **Authentic > polished** (relatable wins)
- **Trend participation** (sounds, formats, challenges)
- **Satisfying content** (ASMR-style organization)
- **POV format** (first-person relatable moments)
- **Fast-paced** (15-60 seconds ideal)

---

| Pillar | Mix | TikTok Angle |
|--------|-----|--------------|

# VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES

## What Works on TikTok

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Hook in first 2 seconds | Slow intros |
| Text overlays throughout | Walls of text |
| Trending sounds | Generic music |
| Authentic/casual | Overly polished |
| Show the product working | Just talk about it |
| Fast cuts | Long static shots |
| Relatable problems | Corporate messaging |

## Recording Tips

- **Screen recordings:** Use phone screen record for authentic feel
- **Face cam:** Adds personality, increases engagement
- **Green screen:** Good for reaction-style content
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts > fancy transitions
- **Text:** Large, readable, on-screen for 2-3 seconds minimum

## Sound Strategy

- Check trending sounds daily
- Save sounds for later use
- Original audio for tutorials/demos
- Trending sounds for trend participation
- Satisfying sounds for ASMR-style content

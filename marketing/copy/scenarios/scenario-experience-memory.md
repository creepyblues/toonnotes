## Biographer Trigger Types

### Time-Based
- **Anniversary:** "One year ago today..."
- **Milestone:** "This is your 100th entry about [topic]"
- **Seasonal:** "Last winter, you wrote about..."
- **Life chapter:** "5 years since you moved to [city]"

### Context-Based
- **Emotional echo:** Current mood matches past mood
- **Topic return:** Writing about same subject
- **Growth moment:** About to do something you were once scared of

### Gratitude Triggers
- **Progress recognition:** "You worried about X. It worked out."
- **Relationship growth:** "Your first entry about [person]..."
- **Skill development:** "Remember when [skill] felt impossible?"

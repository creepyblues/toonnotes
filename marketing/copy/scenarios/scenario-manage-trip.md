# Scenario 1: "The Trip That Planned Itself"
## MODE: MANAGE | Agent: Manager

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-20
**Campaign:** v2-mode-launch

---

## Target Pain Point

> "I have 50 scattered notes about my Japan trip but no actual plan."

---

## User Profile

**Name:** Sarah
**Archetype:** Trip Planner / Note Hoarder
**Behavior:** Saves travel recommendations from TikTok, friends, and articles but never consolidates them into an actual itinerary.

---

## The Story

### Scene 1: The Chaos (Before)

Sarah has been planning her Japan trip for months. Her ToonNotes app is full of scattered notes:

```

```

**Visual:** Grid of note cards in various colors, scattered, no clear organization. Some have travel labels, some don't.

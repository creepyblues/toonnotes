# EMAIL DESIGN GUIDELINES

## Layout

- Clean, minimal design
- Single column
- Mobile-first (most opens on mobile)
- One primary CTA button per email
- ToonNotes brand colors (teal accents)

## Structure

```
[Header: ToonNotes logo, minimal]

[Greeting]

[Hook/Opening ‚Äî 1-2 sentences]

[Main content ‚Äî scannable, short paragraphs]

[CTA button]

[Closing]

[Signature]

[P.S. ‚Äî often the most-read part]

[Footer: Unsubscribe, address]
```

## Formatting

- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold for emphasis (sparingly)
- Emojis in subject lines (test)
- No emojis in body (unless matching v2 tone)
